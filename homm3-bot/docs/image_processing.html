image_processing.detecting_window.skills_window(h)[source]

Very crude, yet working solution for our skills picking needs for our heroes. First, it checks for an ‘okey’ sign on our screen. After detection, if there indeed is an ‘okey’ here - we check certain positions and use optical character recognition on them to get our skills’ names. Then we pick the best skill according to our calculations and click it on our screen in game. Parameter h is our hero and it gets updated with picked skill.

Parameters

h – our Hero that gets updated with new skill
